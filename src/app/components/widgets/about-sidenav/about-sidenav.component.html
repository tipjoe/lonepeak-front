About this Group ▼

We are residents of the homes near Lone Peak Park (i.e. the skate park) working to make our neighborhood more friendly, inclusive, beautiful, and safe.

Everyone who lives here can join. Doing so connects you to neighborhood news, messages, events, and conversations. Any neighbor with ideas for improving our community can use this group to connect with others.
