Who are the Lone Peak Park Neighbors?

We're the people you live by (see map below). This group is a new way to give a crap about each other!

This means having new ways to meet, chat, and do stuff together... on your terms. We want you just the way you are!

Events

Anyone with an idea can plan an event, big or small. Here are some events we've held.

  • Spring and fall cleanup (for Sandy City bulk waste)
  • Neighborhood barbecues
  • Outdoor movie nights
  • Community education
  • Hobby meetups (arts, crafts, biking, gaming... whatever)
  • Kid connects - help your kids find other kids their age
  • Special needs meetups - Special Olympics basketball, etc.
