Who are we?

We're the people next door.

We want our neighborhood to feel more welcoming, friendly, beautiful, and safe for everyone who lives here!

Anyone who joins is free to communicate, plan events, or create groups around shared interests. Just keep it legal and friendly and you're good.

Over the past couple of years, we've held various events.

  • Spring and fall cleanup
  • Barbecues
  • Outdoor movie nights
  • Meetups (hobbies, learning, sports, etc.)

This group is not sponsored by a business or organization. Only members can see what's shared.

Joe Tippetts, a neighbor since 2005, created this group and app. If you have any questions, feel free to contact him at lppneighbors@gmail.com.
